About

Medicine with warmth, precision, and trust.

Dr. Bayan Abunar is a board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist who believes exceptional care begins with listening. Her practice spans the full scope of women's health — from routine wellness and prevention to complex obstetric and gynecologic care.

Beyond the clinic, she is co-founder of ObMob, an education platform helping OB/GYN physicians prepare for board certification — a reflection of her commitment to raising the standard of care through better-trained physicians.

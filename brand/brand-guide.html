The one-page source of truth. Website, Instagram, thumbnails, business cards — everything should follow this, so her presence looks like one person, everywhere.

The essence

Navy + gold. Warm, credible, human.

Classic and authoritative — the navy and serif signal a real, board-certified physician — but never cold. The warmth, the gold, and her humor keep it human. Inspired by her NHSC-scholar identity.

A board-certified OB-GYN sharing the honest, useful, and funny truth about medicine and motherhood.

Typography

Serif for the emotional and identity lines; sans for the functional text. Don't add a third font.

Logo & mark

Wordmark & monogram

The wordmark leads on the site; the "BA" monogram is the profile-photo fallback, favicon, and highlight-cover motif. Never stretch, recolor, or add effects.

Photography

Real over perfect

  • Warm, natural light; navy, cream, or clean background so the palette holds.
  • The same profile photo across website, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube.
  • On camera: good light, clean background, head-and-shoulders framing.
  • Stock "doctor with clipboard" clichés.
  • AI-generated faces or avatars.
  • Anything that looks generic or staged-stiff.

Voice & tone

Warm, funny, credible

Talks to you, not down to you. Her signature is the résumé-vs-real-life contrast.

Would a smart, tired friend who happens to be an OB-GYN say it this way?

  • Always: honest, useful, human.
  • Ends clinical content with "educational, not medical advice."
  • Self-deprecating and specific beats polished and vague.
  • Preachy, fear-mongering, or jargon-heavy.
  • Fake-perfect or shaming.
  • Clickbait she can't back up.

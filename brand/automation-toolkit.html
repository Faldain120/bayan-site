Automation & AI Toolkit

Remove the friction, not the person.

You can automate ~70% of the busywork — editing, captions, scheduling, posting — but not the taste. For a physician, the authentic layer is the brand.

The pipeline

How it fits together

She films; the tools handle the rest. Don't chase AI-avatar or faceless tools — they'd nuke the trust that makes a doctor-creator work.

Batch-film (~90 min/wk)→ AI edit + auto-caption→ Captions/hooks (she edits)→ Schedule the week→ Auto-publish M/W/F

Repurposing & ideas

Only when she needs it

Opus Clip / Klap

Long → short clips

Skip for now. She films short Reels directly. Revisit only if she starts a podcast or YouTube (~$14–29/mo).

ChatGPT / Claude

Hooks & captions

Brainstorm hooks, draft captions — she always edits to sound like her. Add Flick (~$14/mo) only if hashtags become a time-sink.

Hard limits

What NOT to automate (for a physician)

These break trust

  • AI avatars / voice clones / faceless content — the opposite of what a doctor-creator needs.
  • Auto-generated medical "facts" — anything clinical must be her verified knowledge.
  • Robotic auto-DM / auto-comment bots — real replies build the early community (bots are bannable).

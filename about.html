Meet Dr. Bayan Abunar

Board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist, educator, and co-founder — dedicated to women's health and to training the physicians who provide it.

Dr. Bayan Abunar is a board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist whose practice is grounded in a simple belief: exceptional medicine begins with genuinely listening to patients. She provides the full scope of women's health care — from preventive wellness and family planning to obstetric and gynecologic care — always pairing clinical rigor with warmth.

Great care is equal parts science and trust. My patients should leave every visit feeling heard, informed, and confident in the plan.

A passion for education

Dr. Abunar is co-founder of ObMob, a platform that helps OB/GYN physicians prepare for the ABOG oral board examination through realistic, AI-guided practice. Her work there reflects a broader mission: improving women's health outcomes by raising the standard of physician training.

She also shares approachable women's-health education with a growing audience across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube — demystifying pregnancy, gynecologic health, and life as a physician.
